These are the most famous spells in the
wizarding world.
You should know!

1. Expelliarmus

The Disarming Charm. This spell forces an opponent to drop whatever they are holding. It's often used in duels.

2. Lumos

Lights up the tip of the caster's wand, acting like a flashlight. Useful in dark places.

3. Alohomora

Unlocks doors and windows that are not protected by magic. Often used by students sneaking around Hogwarts.

4. Wingardium Leviosa

Makes objects fly or levitate. Requires the correct wand movement and pronunciation!

5. Expecto Patronum

Summons a Patronus, a powerful protective charm against Dementors. It takes the form of an animal unique to the caster.
