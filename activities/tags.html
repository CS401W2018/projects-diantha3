3. What are special entity characters?
Special entity characters are the characters that are not provided on the standard keyboard and to prevent the error in the browser.
For the example is to prevent the mix of the less or greater than signs for te text with less or greater than signs for the tags.

4. What are phrase tags?
HTML phrase tags are unique purpose tags created to define the structural meaning while want to modify or emphasize text.

5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?
OL and UL have the same purpose to make a list on the page. The difference between them is that OLused when we want to make a list with in order points like number or alphabet. However, UL used when we want to make a list using bullet points.
