1. What is normal flow in HTML?

The normal flow in HTML is the way to laying out the elements on an HTML page.

2. What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?
Float help the content to be placed an element on the left or right side of its container. It's to prevent the potential of overlapping.

3. What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?
Position absolute makes the elements positioned absolutely to its first position. Postion relative makes the element positioned relatively to its normal position. Position fixed makes the picture or content stay in the same position eventhough we scroll the page. Absolute and fixed usually used for header/nav/footer

4. How can you make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices?
By applying the absolute position or fixed psotion. To help the user easily find it.
